Title: Summary comparison - completeness policy

Objective
Evaluate which summary more completely captures important information.

Instructions
You are given 2 summaries (A, B) created from a reference text. Evaluate which summary more completely captures important information.

Scoring Criteria
A significantly better than B (Score: -2)
A slightly better than B (Score: -1)
Neither A or B is better than the other (Score: 0)
B slightly better than A (Score: 1)
B significantly better than B (Score 2)
